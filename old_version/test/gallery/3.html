PAN IIT

With the aim of increasing the outreach of NSS, IIT Roorkee, a team of seven members headed by the program officer, Dr. Smita Jha, went to the PAN IIT 2010 Conclave held at Greater Noida. An exhibit displaying the works of NSS was put up by the members of Team NSS and handouts containing the works of NSS, IIT Roorkee, were distributed among the visitors to the IIT Roorkee stall. The works got appreciation not only from IIT Roorkee alumni but also from other people who visited IIT Roorkee stall. A presentation on Sustainable Economic Development was also given by Samya Mishra (Deputy General Secretary of NSS) in the alumni interaction session of IIT Roorkee. All the new IITs also agreed to join hands with NSS, IIT Roorkee in its quest for the upliftment of the society.
