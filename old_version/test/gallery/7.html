ON CAMPUS

PRERNA

Provides computer education, tuition and academic help to the students in and around the campus who need it due to the lack of basic academic infrastructure.

HEALTH CELL

Organizes blood donation camps, yoga camps, skits, lectures and workshops to impart health awareness to people and also to educate the underprivileged about the importance of hygiene and the basic necessities for a healthy life.

NIRMAN

Provides basic education to the children of the poor construction workers employed at the various construction sites in the college. It holds regular classes and takes the children out for educational games and inspirational movies for their psychological and physical upliftment.

HUMAN WELFARE & ENVIRONMENT CELL

Conducts the general welfare activities like newspaper collection drives and the raising of relief funds etc. and co-ordinates with other welfare organisations in the region to conduct mass scale programmes.

SOCIAL AWARENESS CELL

Organizes skits, guest lectures, debates, seminars, quizzes, workshops and other such events to make the people aware of the prevailing problems in our society and suggest their solutions.

WEB & PUBLICITY CELL

Handles all the web related issues of NSS IITR. Aims at maintaining media relations. Seeks tie up of NSS with different NGOs. At a local level assisting other cells in publicising their event.

TECHNOCREATIVE CELL

The work of TechnoCreative cell largely depends upon the activities organized by other cells of NSS. This cell was formed with the primary objective of being the public face of NSS IITR. In addition, there was a need to maintain the track of all the work being done in NSS.

FINANCE

An Integral part of the NSS team the Finance Cell assumes the duty of meeting the monetary requirements of the various activities that are conducted under the canopy of NSS.
