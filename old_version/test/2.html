NSS IITR was established in the 1970's but was reintroduced later with many new features and improvements. The students of IITR along with the faculty advisors give their best to contribute to the betterment of the society. Thus, aiming the progress of the nation at large. The selfless and dedicated service provided by not only the students of NSS, but also other volunteers of this great institution is commendable. NSS IITR aims at overall development of the society and best efforts are made so that no area is left out. The range of services provided by students varies greatly. Of the plethora of activities we undertake a few major include teaching of school students who cannot afford a good education both inside and outside the campus, spreading social awareness through lectures, debates etc. and organizing blood donation and first-aid camps. Our work also includes setting up of small scale industries for the upliftment of rural areas nearby. Our work covers a lot of villages and we aim at covering the entire state of Uttarakhand.
